Rainfall info automation

Surge pricing, the practice of hiking up ride-hailing fares in times of high demand, usually kicks in when it rains. This puts consumers at a disadvantage. Insurance companies offer insurance to customers against surge pricing. In this project, to help insurance companies monitor whether claims are valid, or to do dynamic pricing, I automated the information on how heavy the rainfall was at various locations at any given point in time.
